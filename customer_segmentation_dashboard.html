Key insights & recommended actions
High-Value Loyalists drive 58% of revenue despite being 32% of customers. Prioritize VIP retention programs and early access offers.
Price-Sensitive Buyers respond 3× better to discount campaigns. Bundle deals and loyalty points can shift them up-funnel.
Occasional Browsers have high cart-abandonment. Re-engagement emails with personalized product picks can lift conversion by ~22%.
New & Exploratory customers are growing fastest (+31%). Onboarding flows and first-purchase incentives are critical conversion levers.
